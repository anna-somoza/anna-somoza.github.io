About me

I completed my PhD at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and Universiteit Leiden, supervised by Joan-Carles Lario and Marco Streng.

My research interests are in algorithmic number theory and arithmetic geometry, and I am currently working on the construction of curves of low genus.
