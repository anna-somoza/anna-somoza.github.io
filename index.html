About me

My research interests are in algorithmic number theory and arithmetic geometry, and I am currently working on the construction of superelliptic curves.

I'm currently a PostDoc at the IRMAR, in Université de Rennes 1. I completed my PhD at UPC (Barcelona) and Universiteit Leiden in March 2020.
